About us

From a local meat market to online orders.

Baltic Pork Market continues the work of a former Latvian meat market by helping customers request fresh pork products, smoked meats and sausages online.

Our story

A familiar meat market, adapted for today.

The business started with a physical meat market in Latvia, serving local customers with fresh pork products, smoked meats and sausages. After the shop closed, the goal became simple: continue serving customers in a more flexible way through online order requests.

This website is built to make ordering easier. Customers can browse products, send a request, and receive confirmation about availability, final weight, price, pickup or local delivery.

The focus remains the same: clear communication, honest confirmation before payment, and reliable pork products for everyday meals, family tables and BBQ.

What matters

Built around trust, not complicated checkout.

Meat orders are different from standard online products. Weight, availability and preparation can change, so every order is confirmed manually before completion.

How we work

Order request first. Confirmation second.

This approach avoids wrong prices and unavailable products. Instead of forcing customers through an automatic checkout, the business confirms the details manually.

1. Customer chooses products

Fresh pork, smoked products, sausages, BBQ products or meat boxes.

2. Customer sends a request

The request includes products, approximate quantities and contact details.

3. The order is confirmed

Availability, final weight, price and pickup or delivery details are confirmed clearly.

4. The customer receives the products

The order is prepared and handed over through pickup or local delivery.

Our promise

Simple, honest and practical.

The goal is not to look like a luxury meat brand if the business is not one. The goal is to be clear, trustworthy and useful: show the products, explain the ordering process, confirm details properly and make it easy for customers to come back.
