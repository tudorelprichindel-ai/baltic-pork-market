About Baltic Meat Market

A local meat market experience, moved online.

Baltic Meat Market helps customers in Latvia request pork, beef, lamb, chicken, BBQ products, smoked products and meat boxes through a simple online ordering flow.

Our purpose

Simple meat ordering without unclear checkout.

Meat products are different from standard online shop products. Weight, availability, preparation day and final price can vary. That is why Baltic Meat Market uses an order-request system: customers send what they want, then receive confirmation before pickup or local delivery.

What we focus on

Clear categories, clear confirmation, local service.

The goal is not to create a complicated supermarket. The goal is to keep the practical service of a local meat market and make it easier for customers to request products online.

Why request-based ordering?

Because meat orders need confirmation.

A fixed checkout can create mistakes when product weight or availability changes. Request-based ordering keeps the process realistic: the customer asks, the business checks, and only then the order is confirmed.

See how ordering works
Fresh availability Products are confirmed based on current stock and preparation schedule.
Final weight The customer receives the final weight before the final price is confirmed.
Clear communication Questions can be handled before pickup or delivery.
Better customer trust The process avoids surprise substitutions, unclear prices and automatic mistakes.
