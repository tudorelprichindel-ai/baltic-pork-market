Our story

A familiar service, adapted for online orders.

Gaļas grozs started with a physical meat shop in Latvia. That experience shaped a straightforward way of serving customers: show the selection clearly, understand what the customer needs and confirm the details personally.

The online catalog continues that approach. It makes browsing and preparing an order easier while keeping the final confirmation direct and practical.
