How we work

Clear order request, manual confirmation

The process is designed to avoid confusion between estimated prices and final confirmed prices.

01

Customer builds the cart

Products can be added to cart from the product page or from featured sections on the homepage.

02

Preferences are selected

The customer chooses pickup or delivery preference and preferred payment method.

03

Request is sent on WhatsApp

The cart becomes a structured WhatsApp message with products, estimated total and preferences.

04

Seller confirms final details

Product availability, exact weight, final total and payment details are confirmed before payment.

Why this model works

Better for products sold by weight

Meat orders are different from fixed-price packaged products. Exact weight can vary, availability can change and final totals often need confirmation. The website gives the customer a clear estimate without pretending that every order can be finalized automatically.

Less confusion Customers know that the website total is an estimate before final confirmation.
More flexibility Pickup, delivery and payment method can be confirmed depending on the order.
Practical for the seller The seller receives a clear request without needing a full online checkout system at this stage.
