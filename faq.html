Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Open a question to see the answer. If your situation is different, contact us directly on WhatsApp.

How do I place an order?

Add the products you need to the cart, choose your pickup or delivery preference and send the prepared request on WhatsApp. Availability, exact weight and the final total are confirmed before payment.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can browse the catalog, build your cart and send the request without creating an account.

Are the prices final?

Prices shown on the website are indicative. The final total is confirmed after product availability and exact weight are checked.

Why can the final weight be different?

Fresh meat pieces can vary in size and weight. The amount displayed in the cart is an estimate until the selected products are weighed and confirmed.

What happens if a product is unavailable?

The seller confirms current availability before payment. You can then adjust the request directly on WhatsApp if something is not available.

Can I choose pickup or delivery?

Yes. Select your preference in the cart. The practical details and availability of the chosen option are confirmed directly with the seller.

How is payment arranged?

You can select a preferred payment method in the cart. The available method and payment details are confirmed after availability, exact weight and the final total are established.

Can I change the request after sending it?

Yes. Changes can be discussed on WhatsApp before the seller gives the final confirmation and payment is arranged.
