General questions

Ordering with Gaļas grozs

The website helps customers prepare a clear order request before final confirmation on WhatsApp.

01

Can I order directly from the website?

You can build your cart on the website and send the order request on WhatsApp. The seller confirms final availability, exact weight and total price before payment.

02

Are the prices final?

Prices shown on the website are indicative. The final total can change depending on exact product weight, availability and final confirmation with the seller.

03

Why are prices indicative?

Fresh meat is often sold by exact weight. Product pieces can vary in size, so the final weight and total price must be confirmed manually.

04

What products are available?

The main categories are pork, beef, chicken, BBQ & Grill products and meat boxes. Availability is confirmed manually after the request is sent.

Cart and WhatsApp

How the order request is sent

The cart creates a structured WhatsApp message so the seller can quickly understand the request.

05

What does the WhatsApp message include?

It includes selected products, category, quantity, indicative price, estimated total, pickup or delivery preference and preferred payment method.

06

Can I choose pickup or delivery?

Yes. In the cart you can choose pickup, delivery or to be confirmed. If you choose delivery, you can add the address or delivery details before sending the request.

07

Can I change the order after sending it?

Yes. Because the order is confirmed manually on WhatsApp, changes can be discussed directly with the seller before final confirmation.

08

Do I need to create an account?

No. The website works without customer accounts. The order request is sent directly through WhatsApp.

Payment

Payment is arranged after confirmation

The customer can choose a preferred payment method in the cart, but final payment details are agreed after confirmation.

09

How can I pay?

You can select a preferred payment method in the cart: cash, bank transfer, online payment link if available, or to be confirmed.

10

When is payment arranged?

Payment is arranged after the seller confirms product availability, exact weight, final total and pickup or delivery details.

11

Can I request bank transfer?

Yes. You can select bank transfer as your preferred payment method. The seller provides payment details after confirmation.

12

Can I request an online payment link?

Yes, if available. You can select online payment link as your preferred method, and the seller can confirm whether this option is available for the order.
