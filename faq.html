Frequently asked questions

Questions before placing a meat order.

Answers about product availability, estimated prices, cart requests, pickup, local delivery and manual confirmation.

Ordering

How the order request works.

The website does not use automatic payment. Customers add products to the cart, send a request, and receive confirmation before the order is completed.

Can I order directly from the website?

You can create an order request from the website. Add products to the cart, check the estimated total, then send the request. Final availability, weight and price are confirmed manually.

Is the cart total final?

No. The cart total is informative. Meat products can vary by final weight, availability and preparation schedule, so the final price is confirmed manually.

Why not use automatic checkout?

Because meat products are often sold by final weight. Automatic checkout can create wrong totals if the exact product weight changes.

Do I need to write the product list manually?

No. Add products to the cart and send the request from there. The cart creates the product list automatically.

Products

What products can I request?

You can request pork, beef, lamb, chicken, BBQ & grill products, smoked products and meat boxes. Some products may depend on stock, preparation day and current availability.

Availability

Product availability can change.

The catalogue shows what customers can request, but not every product is guaranteed to be available at all times.

Are all products always available?

No. Availability depends on stock and preparation schedule. The order is confirmed only after availability is checked.

Can I request several meat categories in one order?

Yes. You can add pork, beef, lamb, chicken, BBQ products, smoked products and meat boxes into the same cart request.

Can I request a product that is not listed?

Yes, but it should be confirmed manually. Use the contact page to ask about special requests.

Can product weight be different from what I request?

Yes. Meat cuts can vary. The business confirms the final weight before confirming the final price.

Pickup & delivery

How do I receive the order?

Pickup and local delivery details are confirmed after the product request is reviewed.

Is pickup available?

Pickup details are confirmed after the order request is reviewed and the products are available.

Is local delivery available?

Local delivery may be available depending on location, order size and schedule. Details are confirmed manually.

When is the order prepared?

Preparation depends on the product type, availability and agreed pickup or delivery time.

Can I choose a preferred date?

Yes. Mention your preferred date when sending the request. It will be confirmed if possible.

Prices

Estimated prices are not final prices.

Prices shown in the cart help customers estimate the order value. The final price is confirmed after product availability and final weight are checked.

Confirmation

Before the order is completed.

The customer receives final confirmation before pickup or delivery.

What happens after I send the request?

The product list is reviewed. Then availability, final weight, final price and pickup or delivery details are confirmed.

Am I forced to buy after sending the request?

No. The request starts the confirmation process. The final order is agreed after details are confirmed.

Can the order be changed after confirmation?

Changes may be possible before preparation starts. This depends on timing and product availability.

What if a product is unavailable?

The business can suggest removing it from the order or replacing it with another available product.
