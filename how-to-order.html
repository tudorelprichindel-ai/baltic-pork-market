Ordering process

How to order fresh pork products.

Send an order request, receive confirmation, and choose pickup or local delivery. Every order is confirmed manually before completion.

Important

This is not automatic checkout.

Meat products are often sold by final weight. Product availability can also change depending on preparation day and current stock. That is why every order is confirmed manually.

01

Choose your products

Browse fresh pork, smoked pork, sausages, BBQ products and meat boxes.

02

Send an order request

Tell us the products you want, approximate quantity, your name and phone number.

03

We check availability

We verify what is currently available and what can be prepared for your order.

04

We confirm final weight and price

You receive the final confirmed price before payment or pickup.

05

Pickup or local delivery

The order is prepared and handed over through the agreed method.

What to include

Send a clear request.

A vague message creates delays. A clear message helps the order get confirmed faster.

Product names

Example: pork neck, smoked bacon, grill sausages, BBQ box.

Approximate quantity

Example: 2 kg pork neck, 1 kg smoked bacon, 2 packs sausages.

Your contact details

Name, phone number and WhatsApp if available.

Pickup or delivery

Tell us if you prefer pickup, local delivery, or if you are not sure yet.

Questions

Common order questions

Why is the final price confirmed manually?

Because meat products can vary by final weight. Manual confirmation prevents wrong totals.

Can I order products that are not listed?

Yes. You can ask about other products and we will confirm if they are available.

Is delivery available?

Local delivery options depend on location and order size. Details are confirmed before the order is completed.

Can I order in Latvian or Russian?

Latvian and Russian versions are planned. For now, order support depends on the available contact method.
