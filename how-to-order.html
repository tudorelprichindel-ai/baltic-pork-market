Ordering process

How to reserve fresh meat products.

Choose pork, beef, lamb, chicken or mixed meat products, send an order request, and receive clear confirmation before pickup or local delivery.

Important

This is a request-based ordering system.

Meat products are not always suitable for automatic checkout because availability, final weight and price can change. Manual confirmation keeps the order clear and honest.

Availability is checked first Final weight is confirmed Final price is confirmed Pickup or delivery is agreed
01

Choose your products

Browse pork, beef, lamb, chicken, BBQ products, smoked products or meat boxes.

02

Send an order request

Send the product names, approximate quantity, your name and phone or WhatsApp number.

03

We check availability

Products can depend on stock, preparation day and what is currently available.

04

We confirm weight and price

The final price is confirmed after the product weight and availability are checked.

05

Pickup or local delivery

The order is prepared and handed over through the agreed pickup or local delivery option.

Clear requests save time

What to include in your message

A vague message creates delays. A clear order request helps the business confirm your order faster.

Product category

Example: pork, beef, lamb, chicken, BBQ, smoked products or meat boxes.

Product names

Example: pork neck, beef ribs, lamb chops, chicken breast or BBQ box.

Approximate quantity

Example: 2 kg pork neck, 1 kg minced beef, 1 kg lamb chops, 2 kg chicken breast.

Contact details

Name, phone number and WhatsApp if available.

Pickup or delivery

Tell us if you prefer pickup, local delivery or if you are not sure yet.

Preferred date

Tell us when you would like to receive or pick up the order if you already know.

Pickup & delivery

Details are confirmed before completion.

Pickup and local delivery can depend on location, order size and preparation schedule. This is why the website collects a request first instead of forcing automatic checkout.

Pickup Customers can request pickup details after products and availability are confirmed.
Local delivery Delivery options depend on location and are confirmed before the order is completed.
Final confirmation The customer receives final product availability, weight and price before completion.

Example

A good order request looks like this.

Hello Baltic Meat Market,

I would like to request an order:

  • 2 kg pork neck
  • 1 kg minced beef
  • 1 kg lamb chops
  • 2 kg chicken breast

Pickup or delivery: local delivery if available.

Please confirm availability, final weight and price.

Ready

Send your order request.

Choose your products and contact us with your list. We will confirm the details before completion.
