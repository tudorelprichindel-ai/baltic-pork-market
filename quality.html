Order process

How the order request works

The process is designed for products sold by weight, where the final amount must be confirmed manually.

01

Browse products

Open the product page and choose from pork, beef, chicken, BBQ & Grill products and meat boxes.

02

Add products to cart

Add products to the cart. The website will show an estimated total based on indicative prices.

03

Choose pickup or delivery

In the cart, select pickup, delivery or to be confirmed. For delivery, add the address or delivery details.

04

Select payment preference

Choose cash, bank transfer, online payment link if available, or to be confirmed.

05

Send request on WhatsApp

The cart is converted into a WhatsApp message with products, estimated total and customer preferences.

06

Confirm final details

The seller confirms availability, exact weight, final price, pickup or delivery details and payment method.

Why confirmation is needed

Fresh meat is usually sold by exact weight

Meat products can vary by piece size and availability. The cart gives a clear estimate, but the final order total is confirmed manually after the seller checks the exact products.

Indicative prices Prices help customers understand the expected cost before sending a request.
Manual confirmation Final weight, availability and total price are confirmed directly on WhatsApp.
Flexible payment Payment is arranged after confirmation by the method agreed with the customer.
