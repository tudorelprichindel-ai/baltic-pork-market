Quality & confirmation

Clear confirmation before every order.

Meat products can vary by availability, final weight and preparation schedule. That is why every order request is checked and confirmed manually before completion.

Our approach

No blind checkout. No unclear final price.

Baltic Meat Market uses a request-based ordering system because meat products are not always suitable for fixed automatic checkout. Customers can choose products, add them to the cart, see an estimated price, and then send an order request for final confirmation.

What gets confirmed

Four details matter before the order is completed.

The goal is to avoid confusion between what the customer requests and what is actually available.

01

Availability

Products are checked based on current stock and preparation schedule.

02

Final weight

Meat cuts can vary, so final weight is confirmed before the final price.

03

Final price

Cart prices are informative. The final price is confirmed manually.

04

Pickup or delivery

Pickup and local delivery details are agreed before completion.

Why estimated prices?

Because real meat orders depend on weight.

The cart helps customers build an order without typing everything manually. But the price shown in the cart is still an estimate. The final amount depends on the confirmed product weight and current availability.

Product categories

The same confirmation process applies to all categories.

Pork, beef, lamb, chicken, BBQ products, smoked products and meat boxes can all be requested through the cart.

02

03

Lamb leg, shoulder, chops, ribs, minced lamb and lamb stew cuts.

04

Chicken

Chicken breast, thighs, wings, drumsticks, whole chicken and marinated products.

Important note

The cart is not final payment.

The cart is an order request tool, not an automatic payment system. Customers use it to prepare a clear request. The final confirmation is still done manually, so availability, weight, price, pickup and delivery details remain accurate.
